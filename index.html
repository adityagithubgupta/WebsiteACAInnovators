Our Services

CAD Conversions

Simplify design updates, streamline workflows, and improve project accuracy with our reliable and efficient CAD conversion services. Whether you're digitizing old drawings, transitioning between software, or standardizing file formats, we ensure precision and quality every step of the way.

Our CAD Conversion Services

Image to CAD Conversion: Transform low-detail or conceptual images into highly accurate CAD models. Perfect for sketches, scanned documents, or raster images, this service enables engineers and designers to work with detailed, scalable, and editable CAD drawings.

Paper to CAD Conversion: Modernize your legacy documentation by digitizing paper-based designs into precise CAD files. We meticulously recreate dimensions, annotations, and details, ensuring every element is captured for seamless integration into modern workflows.

PDF to CAD Conversion: Convert static PDF files into fully editable CAD designs. This service is ideal for making design modifications, adding new features, or updating layouts without starting from scratch.

CAD to CAD Conversion: Streamline collaboration and compatibility by adapting files between different CAD formats. Whether transitioning between software platforms or meeting specific client requirements, we ensure file integrity, proper scaling, and format compatibility.

CAD Modeling

Unlock the potential of your designs with our expert CAD modeling services. We deliver precise and versatile CAD models tailored to your project needs. From concept to production, our expertise spans multiple modeling techniques, ensuring designs are robust and manufacturable.

Our Mechanical CAD modeling Services:

Solid Modeling: We craft 3D solid models with a focus on functional and structural integrity. These models provide accurate representations of your products, allowing for detailed analysis, simulation, and testing. Solid modeling is perfect for designing parts and assemblies that require precision and high-performance standards.

Surface Modeling: Our surface modeling services cater to projects requiring intricate, smooth, and aesthetically appealing designs. This technique is ideal for creating complex geometries, ergonomic shapes, and visually striking designs, often used in consumer electronics, automotive exteriors, and medical devices.

Assembly Modeling: We create complete assemblies that simulate the precise interaction of components in your design. Our assembly models ensure that parts fit together seamlessly, function correctly, and are easy to assemble or disassemble during production or maintenance. This is especially useful for machinery, robotics, and multi-component products.

Bill of Materials (BOM): Alongside detailed CAD models, we provide comprehensive Bills of Materials (BOM) for production. Our BOM includes a complete list of materials, components, quantities, and specifications, streamlining procurement and manufacturing processes.

Finite Element Analysis

Enhance product reliability and performance with our advanced Finite Element Analysis (FEA) services. We help predict how your designs will respond to real-world conditions, enabling informed engineering decisions and reducing development risks.

Our Mechanical CAD modeling Services:

Stress / Structural Analysis: Evaluates stress distribution, deformation, and load-bearing capacity to ensure structural integrity and safety.

Static Analysis: Assesses how components perform under steady loads, identifying potential weak points and optimizing material usage.

Linear Analysis: Simulates material behavior under small deformations, providing accurate results for standard engineering applications.

Dynamic Analysis: Examines time-dependent forces such as vibrations, impacts, and cyclic loading to improve durability and performance.

Thermal Analysis: Analyzes heat distribution, thermal stresses, and material responses to temperature changes, essential for electronics, engines, and industrial systems.

CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) Analysis: Simulates fluid flow, pressure distribution, heat transfer, and aerodynamics to optimize designs for efficiency and performance.

Design Optimization: Refines product designs by balancing strength, weight, material selection, and cost for enhanced efficiency and manufacturability.
